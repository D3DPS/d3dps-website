D3DPS 2.0 — SQUARE CHECKOUT + BUSINESS ADMIN
================================================

INCLUDED NOW
- Shopping cart with quantities
- Secure Square-hosted checkout
- Server-side price validation
- Product stock field
- Online purchase toggle and CAD price
- Decap CMS collections for:
  * Products
  * Filament inventory
  * Print queue
  * Orders
  * Customers
  * STL library
  * Expenses

UPLOAD
1. Extract this ZIP.
2. Upload all files and folders to the ROOT of the GitHub repository.
3. Replace matching files when prompted.
4. Confirm netlify/functions/create-square-checkout.mjs exists in GitHub.
5. Wait for Netlify to deploy.

NETLIFY VARIABLES
- SQUARE_ACCESS_TOKEN (secret; Functions scope or All scopes)
- SQUARE_LOCATION_ID (Functions scope or All scopes)

PRODUCT SETUP
In /admin, edit a product and set:
- Online purchase enabled: ON
- Online price (CAD): a number such as 20
- Stock quantity: leave blank for made-to-order/unlimited, or enter a number

IMPORTANT LIMITATION
The Orders collection is currently for manual business tracking. Automatically copying paid Square orders into the CMS requires a Square webhook plus persistent database/storage. That should be implemented as a separate phase after checkout is confirmed working.

TEST
1. Enable online purchase for one low-cost product.
2. Add it to the cart.
3. Click Checkout securely with Square.
4. Confirm Square opens and shows the correct product, quantity, and price.
