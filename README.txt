D3DPS PREMIUM WEBSITE
=====================

HOW TO PREVIEW
1. Right-click the ZIP and choose “Extract All”.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Double-click index.html.
4. Keep index.html, styles.css, script.js, and the assets folder together.

IMPORTANT
Do not open index.html while it is still inside the ZIP. That causes the site to appear as plain text without its styling or logo.

HOW TO ADD YOUR OWN PRODUCT PHOTOS
The website intentionally contains labelled photo placeholders.

Easiest method:
1. Put your edited photos in: assets/products/
2. Use WebP, JPG, or PNG files.
3. Open index.html in Notepad.
4. Find the product you want.
5. Replace the placeholder <div class="product-media placeholder">...</div> with:

   <div class="product-media has-photo">
     <img src="assets/products/YOUR-FILE-NAME.webp" alt="Short product description">
     <button class="favorite-button" type="button" aria-label="Save product">♡</button>
   </div>

Recommended product photo shape: landscape 4:3.
Recommended minimum size: 1200 × 900 pixels.
